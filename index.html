About Me

Hello world! My name is Patrick Hadinata and I am an MS/PhD student at UCLA. I am working under the supervision of Prof. Ertugrul Taciroglu, PhD. My academic interests lie within the intersection of Structural/Earthquake Engineering, Machine Learning, and Hazard Resilience. I aspire to use my research work to make an impact in the society, especially during this climate change era.
